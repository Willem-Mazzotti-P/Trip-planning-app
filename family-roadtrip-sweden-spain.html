Overview

This is a route-first plan for a summer family EV trip from Stockholm to Torrevieja via the Trelleborg-Rostock ferry, then back through France and Germany.

The planning assumptions are simple:

  • 3 children, 2 adults, and a fully loaded car
  • about 500 km nominal EV range, but stops planned with real-world margin
  • preference for large, low-friction charging hubs rather than last-minute city charging
ImportantMain takeaways
  • Order the French Crit’Air sticker before departure.
  • Budget for French motorway tolls and the ferry crossing.
  • Set up multiple charging apps and one backup payment method.
  • Keep the charging plan anchored to the ferry and overnight stops, not just theoretical range.

Before departure

  • Order the French Crit’Air sticker from the official site.
  • Confirm the ferry booking and latest check-in window.
  • Make sure ChargeFinder, ABRP, and at least one backup charging app are logged in and payment-ready.
  • Prefer accommodation with destination charging whenever an overnight stop is involved.

Route-specific itinerary

This itinerary turns the broad Sweden to Spain corridor into one concrete family-first plan for an EV with about 500 km nominal range.

I have kept the user-requested main journey steps as the backbone of the route, then added practical charging hubs so the driving legs stay inside a comfortable real-world buffer rather than stretching the battery to its theoretical maximum.

EV charging strategy

Practical notes

  • Plan around meal stops and ferry timing, not only battery percentage.
  • Prefer large hubs over isolated chargers.
  • Keep at least one backup charging app and one physical payment card ready.
  • In France and Spain, avoid assuming that a foreign EV has automatic access to every low-emission zone.

Useful links

Final advice

This route becomes much easier if you treat Trelleborg, Rostock, Luxembourg, Bourges, Saint-Flour, Sete, Clermont-Ferrand overnight, and Gottingen as rhythm-setting anchors rather than optional charging ideas.
